# ChatGPT Prompt — Generate My TELOS

*Use this prompt in ChatGPT (with memory enabled). The goal is to get ChatGPT to produce a TELOS document from its own memory of you, which can then be compared against and merged with the KnowMe-sourced synthesis.*

---

## The Prompt

---

I want you to produce a TELOS document for me, based on what you already know about me from our prior conversations and your memory.

**What is TELOS?**
TELOS is a structured personal context framework developed by Dan Miessler. It's built on the Greek philosophical concept of *telos* — ultimate purpose or end goal. It organises a person's life and work into seven hierarchical levels, from the deepest problems they care about down to the specific projects they're actually working on.

The seven levels are:

1. **Problems** — The real-world conditions you are genuinely called to address (not theoretical interests — actual felt responsibilities). Prioritise them P0, P1, P2 etc.
2. **Mission** — Your single primary endeavour that addresses your core problem. One sentence.
3. **Narratives** — Self-descriptions at three lengths:
   - N-1: One sentence ("Most Important Sentence") — who you are and what you're doing
   - N-2: 2–3 sentences
   - N-3: One paragraph (origin story)
4. **Goals** — Measurable outcomes you're working toward. Separate professional (GW) from personal (GP).
5. **Challenges** — Specific obstacles preventing progress. Note whether each is internal or external.
6. **Strategies** — Concrete, targeted approaches addressing specific challenges. Not intentions — actual actions.
7. **Projects** — Work currently in progress, each traceable back through a Strategy to a Challenge to a Goal to the Mission.

**What I want:**
Please produce a full TELOS document for me in this structure, drawing only from what you actually know about me from memory. Don't invent or speculate — if you're uncertain about something, flag it as uncertain or leave a gap rather than guessing.

At the end, include:
- A **Traceability Map** showing how each Project connects back through the chain (Project → Strategy → Challenge → Goal → Mission → Problem)
- A **Gaps section** noting where your knowledge is thin and what you'd need to know to complete those levels
